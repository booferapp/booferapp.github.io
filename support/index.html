Frequently Asked Questions

What is Boofer and how does it work? +
Boofer is a privacy-first messaging app. You get a unique virtual phone number and a custom handle. You can chat, call, and video call with other Boofer users without revealing your real identity or phone number. Everything is end-to-end encrypted.
Is Boofer really free? +
Yes! Core features — messaging, voice calls, video calls, file sharing — are completely free. We plan to offer optional premium features in the future, but the core experience will always be free.
How do I find someone on Boofer? +
You can find people by their Boofer handle (@username) or their virtual number. You can also share a profile link — booferapp.github.io/p/theirhandle — which opens directly in the app.
Can I use Boofer without giving my real phone number? +
Absolutely. Boofer does not require your real phone number to register. You only need a username and optionally an email for account recovery. You receive a Boofer virtual number automatically.
What happens if I lose my device? +
You can recover your account using your registered email address. Your messages are end-to-end encrypted, so message history on the old device cannot be transferred (by design, for privacy). Your account, contacts, and profile will be fully restored on your new device.
How do I delete my account? +
Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account within the Boofer app. This permanently deletes your account and all associated data within 30 days. This action is irreversible. If you cannot access the app, email support@booferapp.io.
How do I report someone's profile or message? +
Tap and hold a message or visit a profile and tap the three-dot menu → Report. Our moderation team reviews all reports. You can also email support@booferapp.io with details.
What do the /p/ and /c/ links do? +
booferapp.github.io/p/handle — Opens that user's Boofer profile in the app.

booferapp.github.io/c/handle — Opens a direct chat with that user in the app.

If you don't have Boofer installed, you'll be prompted to download it.
