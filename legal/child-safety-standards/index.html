Last Updated: March 11, 2026

1 Our Safety Standards

Boofer prohibits the use of its platform for the following activities:

  • The creation, distribution, or promotion of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM).
  • Solicitation or grooming of minors.
  • Engaging in sexually explicit conduct involving or targeted at minors.
  • Any behavior that exploits or endangers children.

2 Prevention Practices

To uphold these standards, Boofer implements several layers of protection:

  • Encryption with Safety: While messages are end-to-end encrypted, we use metadata analysis and user reports to identify potential bad actors.
  • Automated Scanning: We use industry-standard hashing technology (where applicable and allowed by privacy laws) to prevent the upload of known CSAM.
  • Age Verification: Boofer is strictly for users aged 13 and older. We use technical signals to identify and remove accounts belonging to under-age users.
  • Community Moderation: Our safety team reviews all reports related to minor safety with highest priority.

3 How to Report Concerns

If you encounter content or a user that violates our child safety standards, please report it immediately:

In-App Reporting:

  1. Tap on the user's profile.
  2. Select Report or Block/Report.
  3. Choose Child Safety Concern as the reason.
  4. Provide any additional details to help our team investigate.

Direct Email Reporting:

4 Legal Compliance

Boofer complies with all relevant national and international child safety laws. We work closely with authorities, including:

  • NCMEC Reporting: We are required by law to report any CSAM we become aware of to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children.
  • Law Enforcement Cooperation: We cooperate with legal requests from authorities to protect children and prosecute offenders.
  • Regional Authorities: We comply with regional safety regulations in every country where Boofer is available.
